ReMetrica is a decade-old risk simulation tool used by actuaries worldwide. The team decided to fundamentally re-build and re-design the software to use best-in-class technology to prepare for the next decade.

I worked in their agile development team, helping with all aspects from the initial user research to front-end development. ReMetrica launched in April 2016 in London and Singapore, and won Actuarial Post’s Software of the Year award.
